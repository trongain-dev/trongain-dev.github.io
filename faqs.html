Getting Started

Who is Trongain?

First non traditional hedge fund in Crypto industry that allows smart investment options for sophisticated investors

What are hedge funds?

Hedge funds are alternative investments using pooled funds that employ different strategies to earn active returns, or alpha, for their investors. Hedge funds may be aggressively managed or make use of derivatives and leverage in both domestic and international markets with the goal of generating high returns (either in an absolute sense or over a specified market benchmark).

What is Defi?

Decentralized finance is an experimental form of finance that does not rely on central financial intermediaries such as brokerages, exchanges, or banks to offer traditional financial instruments, and instead utilizes smart contracts on blockchains, the most common being Ethereum, Tron or Binance Smart Chain.

How does Trongain work?

How to register on Trongain?

It's very simple to register on Trongain. Click on Login/Sign up button. Then enter your email id and new password and click Sign up. That's it!

How to deposit TRX?

After you have logged in successfully into your Trongain account, you will see the option to deposit TRX. On clicking it, you will get the TRX address and QR code for deposit. You can either copy the address and send TRX from your wallet, or you may directly scan the QR from your mobile wallet and send TRX to it. Choose according to your convenience

How to get TGC?

Once you've made the TRX deposit, you will receive the TGC token in 1:1 ratio of your TRX. You shall also get daily ROI in TGC token. This TGC token can be easily exchanged on crypto exchanges for other coins.

Security

How do I switch to a different price plan?

Once you choose a plan, that is locked-up for the maturity period. You can claim your principle funds only after the maturity. Although, you will receive the interests and rewards on daily basis. To change the plan, you have to either wat till the maturiy period, or you can create a new account and make new TRX deposit there.

How can I get my money back?

Your invested TRX is always yours. You shall get your invested TRX amount after the lock-up period finishes. For the safety and continuity of the plan, investors are not allowed to withdraw amidst the lock-up period.

What about the security of my payments?

All the invested TRX and the TGC tokens are stored safely in our cold wallets, following robust industry standard safety protocols. The cold wallets have multisig security protocol.
